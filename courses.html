Push Workouts

Main Workout: Barbell Bench Press: 4 sets x 8-12 reps Overhead Press (or Dumbbell Shoulder Press): 3 sets x 10-15 reps Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 sets x 10-12 reps Dumbbell Lateral Raises: 3 sets x 12-15 reps Tricep Dips: 3 sets x 10-15 reps Tricep Rope Pushdowns (or Tricep Pushdowns with straight bar): 3 sets x 12-15 reps Push-ups (can be weighted for added intensity): 3 sets x to failure Accessory Exercises: 8. Chest Flyes (Cable or Dumbbell): 3 sets x 12-15 reps Front Plate Raises: 3 sets x 12-15 reps Cool Down: Finish with static stretching for the chest, shoulders, and triceps. Hold each stretch for 15-30 seconds. Notes: Adjust the weight based on your fitness level, ensuring that the last few reps of each set are challenging. Rest between sets should be around 60-90 seconds. Focus on proper form and controlled movements to maximize muscle engagement and reduce the risk of injury. Remember, it's important to consult with a fitness professional or healthcare provider before starting a new exercise program, especially if you have any pre-existing conditions or concerns. They can provide personalized advice based on your individual needs and goals.
